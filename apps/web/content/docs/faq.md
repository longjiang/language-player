# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What languages does Language Player support?
Language Player supports **60+ languages** with **207+ language pairs**. You can learn any supported language from any other supported language.

### Is Language Player free?
Language Player offers a **Free** tier with access to 600,000+ videos and basic dictionary features. A **Pro** subscription unlocks full transcripts, more word examples, and advanced features.

### What do L1 and L2 mean?
- **L1** = Your native language (the language you already know)
- **L2** = Your target language (the language you're learning)

## Dictionary

### How do I look up a word?
Click any word in the subtitles or transcript, or use the Dictionary page to search manually.

### Can I save words for later?
Yes! Click the save/bookmark icon on any dictionary entry to add it to your **Saved Words** list for review.

### What are CEFR, HSK, and JLPT?
These are standardized language proficiency frameworks:
- **CEFR** — European languages (A1 beginner → C2 mastery)
- **HSK** — Chinese (Level 1 → 6)
- **JLPT** — Japanese (N5 beginner → N1 advanced)

## Account & Billing

### How do I upgrade to Pro?
Visit the **Go Pro** page from the navigation menu to see pricing plans and upgrade.

### What payment methods are accepted?
Credit/debit cards via Stripe. In-app purchases available on mobile.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Your Pro access continues until the end of your billing period. No refunds for partial months.

### How do I reset my password?
Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page to receive a reset email.
