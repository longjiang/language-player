# EPUB Reader

Read EPUB e-books with interactive word lookup.

## How It Works

Upload or open EPUB files and read them with full dictionary integration. Every word is clickable, and your reading progress is saved.

## Features

- **EPUB Support** — Upload .epub files from your device
- **Chapter Navigation** — Jump between chapters easily
- **Dictionary Integration** — Click any word for definitions
- **Progress Saving** — Your position is remembered across sessions
- **Table of Contents** — Navigate using the book's built-in TOC

## Tips

- Download free EPUB books in your target language from Project Gutenberg
- Start with books at your reading level
- Save new vocabulary while reading for later review
