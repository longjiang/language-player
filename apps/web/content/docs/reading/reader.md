# Reader

Read articles with interactive word lookup and translations.

## How It Works

The Reader lets you read text content in your target language. Import articles, paste text, or browse curated content. Every word is clickable for instant dictionary lookup.

## Features

- **Interactive Text** — Click any word to see definitions
- **Translation Toggle** — Show/hide L1 translations
- **Word Tracking** — Words you've saved are highlighted
- **Progress Saving** — Your reading position is remembered

## Tips

- Start with shorter articles and work up to longer ones
- Save unfamiliar words to your vocabulary list for later review
- Toggle translations off to test your comprehension
