# Pricing & Billing

Language Player offers flexible pricing plans for every learner.

## Plans

### Free
- 600,000+ videos across 207+ languages
- Basic dictionary features
- First 10 lines of transcripts with translation
- 2 word examples per video

### Pro Monthly — $10/month
- Complete interactive transcripts
- Hundreds of word examples
- Full dictionary features
- Auto-renews monthly

### Pro Annual — $90/year
- All Pro features
- Save 25% vs monthly
- Billed annually

### Lifetime — $169 one-time
- All Pro features, forever
- One-time payment
- No recurring charges

## Payment Methods

We accept credit and debit cards via Stripe. In-app purchases are available on mobile (iOS/Android).

## Cancellation

You can cancel anytime. Your Pro access continues until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial periods.
