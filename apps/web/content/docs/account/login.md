# Login & Logout

Sign in to access your saved words, watch history, settings, and Pro features.

## How to Log In

1. Click **Log in** in the top bar (or the user icon, then **Sign in**)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Log in**

If you forget your password, use the **Forgot password?** link on the login page to receive a reset email.

## How to Log Out

1. Click your profile icon in the top bar (your initial or the user icon)
2. Click **Log out**

Logging out clears your local saved words cache. Your data is safe on the server and will reload when you sign back in.

## Staying Logged In

Your session is saved in a secure cookie. You stay logged in until you explicitly log out or clear your browser cookies. Closing the browser does not log you out.

## Troubleshooting

- **Wrong password** — Use **Forgot password?** to reset it
- **No reset email** — Check your spam folder, or contact [jon.long@zerotohero.ca](mailto:jon.long@zerotohero.ca)
- **Redirect loop** — Clear your browser cookies for this site and try again
