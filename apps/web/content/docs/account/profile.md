# Profile

Manage your account information and preferences.

## How It Works

Your profile page shows your account details, subscription status, and learning statistics.

## Features

- **Account Info** — Name, email, and profile picture
- **Subscription** — View your current plan and billing details
- **Learning Stats** — See your activity and progress
- **Language Preferences** — Set your default L1 and L2

## Tips

- Keep your email up to date for password recovery
- Check your subscription status before it renews
- Review your learning stats to stay motivated
