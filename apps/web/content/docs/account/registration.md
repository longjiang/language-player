# Registration & Email Verification

Create a free account to save your progress, build your vocabulary list, and access all free features.

## How to Register

1. Click **Log in** in the top bar
2. Click **Register** at the bottom of the login form
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Click **Create account**

## Email Verification

After registering, you'll receive a verification email. Click the link in the email to verify your account. You need to verify before you can log in.

### Didn't Receive the Email?

- Check your spam or junk folder
- Make sure you entered the correct email address
- Wait a few minutes — some email providers are slow
- If it still doesn't arrive, contact [jon.long@zerotohero.ca](mailto:jon.long@zerotohero.ca)

## Account Benefits

Creating an account gives you:

- **Saved Words** — Build your personal vocabulary list
- **Watch History** — Track what you've watched and resume anytime
- **Synced Settings** — Your preferences follow you across devices
- **Review Mode** — Practice vocabulary with spaced repetition
- **Go Pro** — Upgrade anytime for full transcripts and more features

No credit card required for a free account.
