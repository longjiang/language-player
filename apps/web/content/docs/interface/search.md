# Search

Find videos, channels, and content in your target language.

## How It Works

The search bar lets you search across all available content. Results include videos, TV shows, channels, and dictionary entries.

## Features

- **Video Search** — Find videos by title, topic, or keyword
- **Channel Search** — Discover channels in your target language
- **Dictionary Search** — Look up words directly from search
- **Filters** — Refine results by category, difficulty, or length

## Tips

- Search in your target language for more relevant results
- Use short, specific keywords for better matches
- Combine search with category filters for precise results
