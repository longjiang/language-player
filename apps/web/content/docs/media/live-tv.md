# Live TV

Watch live television broadcasts in your target language.

## How It Works

Live TV streams real-time broadcasts from channels in your target language. Subtitles are available where supported.

## Features

- **Live Channels** — Browse channels by country and language
- **Real-Time Subtitles** — Subtitles display as the broadcast airs
- **Channel Favorites** — Save your preferred channels
- **Schedule** — See what's currently playing

## Tips

- Live TV is great for immersion practice
- Try news channels for clear, standard pronunciation
- Entertainment channels offer more casual, conversational language
