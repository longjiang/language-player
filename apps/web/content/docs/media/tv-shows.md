# TV Shows

Watch full TV series with interactive dual subtitles.

## How It Works

Browse TV shows by title, season, and episode. Each episode includes dual subtitles and the full interactive transcript.

## Features

- **Browse by Show** — Find shows by title, genre, or language
- **Seasons & Episodes** — Navigate through full series
- **Interactive Transcript** — Click any word for definitions
- **Continue Watching** — Pick up where you left off

## Tips

- Binge-watching in your target language builds listening stamina
- Use the transcript to review vocabulary after watching
- Switch between subtitle modes to challenge yourself
