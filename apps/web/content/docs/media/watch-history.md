# Watch History

Review and revisit videos you've watched.

## How It Works

Every video you watch is saved to your watch history. You can revisit any video, see your progress, and continue where you left off.

## Features

- **History List** — Chronological list of watched videos
- **Progress Tracking** — See how much of each video you've watched
- **Quick Resume** — Jump back to any video with one click
- **Clear History** — Remove individual entries or clear all

## Tips

- Use watch history to track your learning consistency
- Revisit videos to reinforce vocabulary
- Clear old entries to keep your history manageable
