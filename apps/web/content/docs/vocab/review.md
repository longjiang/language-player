# Review

Practice your saved vocabulary with spaced repetition.

## How It Works

The Review feature helps you memorize vocabulary through structured review sessions. Words are shown as flashcards, and you rate how well you know each one.

## Features

- **Flashcard Mode** — See the word, recall the meaning
- **Spaced Repetition** — Words appear at optimal intervals
- **Difficulty Rating** — Rate words to adjust review frequency
- **Progress Tracking** — See your learning statistics

## Tips

- Review daily for best results
- Be honest with your difficulty ratings
- Focus on quality over quantity — 10-20 words per session is ideal
