# Saved Words

Manage your personal vocabulary list for review and practice.

## How It Works

Words you save from videos, articles, or the dictionary are collected here. You can review them, see example sentences, and track your learning progress.

## Features

- **Word List** — All saved words with definitions
- **Example Sentences** — See each word used in context
- **Review Mode** — Quiz yourself on saved vocabulary
- **Export/Import** — Transfer your word list between devices

## Tips

- Save words while watching videos to build context-rich vocabulary
- Review your list regularly to reinforce memory
- Focus on high-frequency words first
